## 1. Getting Started

### First Time Login

When your account is created by an Admin, you will receive a **welcome email** at your registered email address.

1. Open the email and click **"Set Your Password"**.
2. You will be taken to the PharmaField app. Enter a password that:
   - Is at least 8 characters long
   - Contains at least one uppercase letter (A–Z)
   - Contains at least one lowercase letter (a–z)
   - Contains at least one number (0–9)
3. Click **Set Password & Continue**.
4. You will be redirected to the login page. Use your **email address** and the password you just set to log in.

> **Link expired?** Welcome links are valid for 24 hours. If your link has expired, contact your Admin to send a new one.

---

### Logging In

1. Open the PharmaField app.
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
3. Tap **Login**.

You will be taken to your personal dashboard.

---

### Logging Out

1. Tap **Settings** (bottom navigation, gear icon).
2. Scroll down and tap **Sign Out**.

---

## 2. Your Dashboard

The dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. What you see depends on your role.

### MR / ASM / ZSM Dashboard

You will see a summary of **your own activity**:

| Card | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| Today's Visits | Number of visits you logged today |
| Doctors Visited | How many doctors you have visited this month |
| Pending Bills | Bills where payment is still outstanding |
| Recent Activity | Your latest visits and reports |

You will also see upcoming doctor **birthdays and anniversaries** so you can stay in touch at the right moment.

### Admin / Super Admin Dashboard

Admins see a **company-wide overview**:

- Total number of active MRs, doctors, and chemists
- Team attendance for today
- Bills and payment summary across the company
- Recent visits and reports from the entire field team

---

## 3. Attendance

Attendance lets you mark yourself as **present** for the day with your live location.

### Marking Attendance (MR / ASM / ZSM / Sales Person)

1. Go to **Attendance** from the bottom navigation.
2. Tap **Check In**.
3. The app will ask for your location — tap **Allow** when prompted.
4. Your check-in time and location are saved automatically.
5. At the end of the day, tap **Check Out** to complete your attendance.

> Your attendance history is shown on the same screen under the **History** tab.

### Viewing Team Attendance (Admin)

Admins see a list of all employees who are present today, along with their check-in times and locations.

---

## 4. Doctors

The Doctors section is a directory of all the doctors your team visits.

### Viewing Doctors

1. Tap **Doctors** in the navigation.
2. You will see a list of all doctors with their name, specialty, and hospital/clinic.
3. Use the **search bar** to find a doctor by name or location.
4. Tap any doctor to see their full profile.

### Doctor Profile includes:
- Name, Specialty, Hospital
- Contact details (phone)
- Birthday and Wedding Anniversary (for relationship management)
- Visit history — every visit your team has made to this doctor
- Notes from previous visits

### Adding a Doctor (MR / ASM / ZSM / Admin)

1. Tap the **+** button on the Doctors screen.
2. Fill in:
   - **Full Name** (required)
   - **Specialty** — e.g., Cardiologist, General Physician (required)
   - **Hospital / Clinic Name** (required)
   - Phone number, Birthday, Anniversary (optional but recommended)
3. Tap **Save**.

### Editing a Doctor

1. Open the doctor's profile.
2. Tap **Edit**.
3. Make your changes and tap **Save**.

---

## 5. Chemists

The Chemists section stores details of all pharmacy shops your team supplies to.

### Viewing Chemists

1. Tap **Chemists** in the navigation.
2. Search by shop name, owner name, or phone number.
3. Tap a chemist to view their full details.

### Chemist Profile includes:
- Shop name and owner name
- Phone number, address, GST number
- Territory they belong to
- Bill history — every bill raised against this shop
- Pending payment amount

### Adding a Chemist (MR / ASM / ZSM / Admin)

1. Tap the **+** button.
2. Fill in:
   - **Shop Name** (required)
   - **Owner Name** (required)
   - **Phone**, **Address**, **GST Number**
   - **Territory** — select the area this chemist belongs to
3. Tap **Save**.

---

## 6. Visits

A visit is a record of you going to meet a doctor or attend to a chemist. Logging visits helps your manager see your daily field activity.

### Viewing Your Visits

1. Tap **Visits** in the navigation.
2. You will see all your past visits, newest first.
3. Tap any visit to see the full details.

### Logging a New Visit

1. Tap the **+** button.
2. Fill in:
   - **Doctor** — select the doctor you visited from the list
   - **Visit Date** — defaults to today
   - **Purpose** — what the visit was for (e.g., product promotion, follow-up)
   - **Notes** — any important details from the conversation
3. Tap **Save Visit**.

> Visits are linked to your profile, so your manager can see a complete picture of your field activity.

### Editing a Visit

1. Open the visit.
2. Tap **Edit**.
3. Make your changes and tap **Save**.

---

## 7. Daily Reports

A daily report is a summary you submit at the end of each working day. It gives your manager a clear view of what you accomplished.

### Submitting a Daily Report

1. Tap **Reports** in the navigation.
2. Tap **New Report**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Date** — defaults to today
   - **Summary** — brief description of your day's work
   - **Doctors visited** — how many doctors you saw
   - **Chemists visited** — how many chemist shops you covered
   - **Samples distributed** — number of product samples given out
   - **Orders taken** — any orders collected during the day
   - **Remarks** — any challenges, feedback, or notes for your manager
4. Tap **Submit Report**.

> You can only submit one report per day. Once submitted, you can view it but not change it.

### Viewing Past Reports

All your submitted reports appear on the Reports screen. Tap any date to read the full report.

**Admins** can see reports from all team members, filtered by employee or date.

## 8. Bills & Payments

Bills are raised against chemist shops when products are supplied. Payments are recorded when the chemist pays against an outstanding bill.

### Bills (Admin / ASM / ZSM only — MR cannot access bills)

#### Viewing Bills

1. Tap **Bills** in the navigation.
2. You will see a list of all bills. Use the search bar to find by bill number or shop name.
3. Tap any bill to see the full details including:
   - Bill number and original bill ID
   - Total amount and amount still due
   - Payment history against this bill

#### Creating a New Bill

1. Tap the **+** button.
2. Fill in:
   - **Bill Number** — your own reference number for this bill (e.g., from your physical bill book)
   - **Chemist** — select the shop this bill is for
   - **Total Amount** (required)
   - **Due Date** — when payment is expected
   - **Notes** (optional)
3. Tap **Create Bill**.

> The app also generates its own internal tracking number automatically. Your bill number (e.g., from your bill book) is stored as the "Original Bill Number."

#### Uploading Bill Images

On any bill's detail page, you can upload photos of the physical bill as evidence.

1. Open the bill.
2. Tap **Upload Images**.
3. Select photos from your device.

---

### Payments

#### Recording a Payment

When a chemist makes a payment, record it here so the outstanding amount updates automatically.

1. Go to **Payments** or open a bill and tap **Record Payment**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Amount** received
   - **Payment Mode** — Cash, Cheque, Bank Transfer, UPI
   - **Reference Number** — cheque number, transaction ID, etc. (optional)
   - **Date** of payment
   - **Notes** (optional)
3. Tap **Save Payment**.

The bill's outstanding balance will reduce automatically.

#### Viewing All Payments

Go to **Payments** in the navigation to see a list of all recorded payments across all bills, with filters for date range and payment mode.

## 9. Settings & Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Tap **Settings** (gear icon in the navigation).
2. Your name, email, role, employee code, and phone number are displayed.

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Under **Change Password**, tap **Change**.
3. Enter your **current password**.
4. Enter your **new password** (must meet the same rules as your initial password).
5. Re-enter the new password to confirm.
6. Tap **Update Password**.

> If you forget your current password, contact your Admin to send you a password reset link via email.

## 10. Admin — Team Management

> This section is only accessible to **Admin** and **Super Admin** roles.

### Viewing the Team

1. Go to **Team** in the navigation.
2. You will see all employees with their name, role, and status (Active / Inactive).
3. Filter by role or search by name, email, or phone.

### Adding a New Team Member

1. Tap **+ Add**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Full Name** (required)
   - **Email Address** (required) — the welcome email will be sent here
   - **Phone Number** (required)
   - **Password** — set an initial password (the employee can change it using the welcome email link)
   - **Role** — choose from MR, ASM, ZSM, Sales Person, Admin
   - **Employee Code** (optional)
   - **Date of Joining** (optional)
3. Tap **Add User**.

The new employee will receive a welcome email with a link to set their own password.

### Editing a Team Member

1. Open the employee's profile.
2. Tap **Edit**.
3. You can update their name, phone, role, employee code, or joining date.
4. Tap **Save Changes**.

> Changing someone's role takes effect immediately — they will see a different navigation and permissions on their next action.

### Resetting an Employee's Password

If an employee is locked out or forgot their password:

**Option 1 — Send a reset link (recommended)**
1. Open the employee's profile.
2. Tap **Send Reset Link**.
3. The employee receives an email with a link to set a new password. The link expires after 24 hours.

**Option 2 — Set a password directly**
1. Open the employee's profile and tap **Edit**.
2. Under **Reset Password**, enter a new password and tap **Reset Password**.
3. Share the new password with the employee securely.

### Activating / Deactivating an Employee

If an employee leaves or needs temporary suspension:

1. Go to the Team list.
2. Tap the toggle (active/inactive icon) next to their name.
3. An inactive employee **cannot log in** until reactivated.

## 11. Admin — Territories

Territories help organize your field team by geography. Each chemist and visit is linked to a territory.

> This section is only accessible to **Admin** and **Super Admin**.

### Viewing Territories

Go to **Territories** to see the full geographic hierarchy:
- **State** → **District** → **City** → **Territory**

### Adding Territories

You can add new states, districts, cities, and territory zones as your business expands into new areas. Each level must be created before you can add the next one below it.
